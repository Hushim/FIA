Detailed Description

This tool simulates the usage of a Highlighter. It has a oval mask with a radius of 7.0 (diameter of 15), an opacity of 0.4 (semi-transparent), an angle of 90 degrees counter-clockwise from the x-axis, and an elongation ratio of 0.3.
